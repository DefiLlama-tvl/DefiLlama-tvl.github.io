Introduction to DefiLlama TVL

DefiLlama TVL is a powerful data aggregation platform that tracks Total Value Locked across the decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem. Known for its transparency and real-time analytics, DefiLlama TVL allows users to explore and compare DeFi protocols across multiple blockchains.

What is DefiLlama?

DefiLlama is an open-source DeFi analytics tool that collects and displays on-chain metrics like TVL, protocol dominance, and usage trends. With its focus on decentralization and accessibility, DefiLlama has become a trusted source for tracking the health of DeFi projects globally.

  • Tracks Total Value Locked across 100+ blockchain ecosystems
  • Free and transparent platform with no account needed
  • Real-time updates for each protocol and chain
  • Covers lending platforms, DEXs, yield aggregators, and bridges
